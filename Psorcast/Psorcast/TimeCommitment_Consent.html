Before you volunteer, it is important to know how much time is involved.

Signing up to volunteer for this study takes about 10 minutes. After that, the study takes about 5-10 minutes each week.

You decide when and how much to participate. You can stop and start your participation at any time.

Transmitting data collected in this study may count against your mobile data plan. You may configure the application to only use Wi-Fi connections to limit the impact this data collection has on your data plan. We estimate that using the app will use 0.003GB data per week)
