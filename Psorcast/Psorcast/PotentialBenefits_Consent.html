The primary benefit of this study is increasing understanding of psoriasis. We will be looking for health patterns across large groups to investigate hypotheses and our ability to make forecasts of psoriasis outcomes.

You may or may not personally benefit from this research study. However, you will contribute to MS research. You will be able to view your own study data over time. This may give you clue about changes in your health and wellness. You can share your data with anyone you choose.
